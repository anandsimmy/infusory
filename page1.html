WORKSHOPS

Scroll down to get excited with the wide variety of workshops we provide !
We got workshops on  Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, 3D Printing, IOT, VFX, Product and Game development and many more.

VR ROBOTICS

Excited about VR? Interested in Robotics? Well, this workshop gets two birds with one stone! Venture into the amazing world of VR by learning to create VR apps and also create a robot in the process. Combine them both and you have a magical formula of a workshop that promises you a few exciting days of learning. Get real time streaming with 360 environment capture into a working robot and visualise it with VR Box, in our VR Robotics Workshop.

3D PRINTING

Enlighten yourself with the magical world of 3D modelling and product design, where you can get real life models of your creations, with 3D Printing. Improve your conceptional design skills, learn about 3D Printers and find the joy in holding your finished model in your hands.

IoT WORKSHOP

Jump abroad the bandwagon of Internet Of Things, which represents the next evolution of Internet, taking a huge leap in its ability to gather, analyse, and distribute data that we can turn into information, knowledge and, ultimately, wisdom. Learn about arduino interfacing, coding, an even app development in our exclusive IoT Workshop.

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Take notice, innovators of tomorrow! Learn the basics of product development and the scope, constraints and design factors of creating a product. Brainstorm your ideas, and stive forward to make a finshed product. Pitch your idea and learn to create a business model, at our Product Development Workshop.

VFX

Visual Effects basically includes the study on the processes by which images are created or manipulated. VFX is one of the top ranking technical advancements mainly used in film making. Here we shoot alive footage(video) and do the work. The basic knowledge we deliver can be expanded to making of next Avatar and Bhahubali. We will teach each and everyone from basics.

VR GAME DEVELOPMENT

Transport yourself into another world by learning about the latest technology on the block, virtual reality. Extent the possibilities by creating a game in a virtual environment, complete with game creation, programming, Google VR, and app development. Sky is the limit, as you design your own games and live inside the world you create! 

AUGMENTED REALITY

Now that you're frequently hearing about the term AR in latest mobile technology, lets make AR apps and games with UNITY. Master game development with AR, and even learn to make it into a finished app for android. Climb abroad the AR hype, with our AR Workshop and make something amazing!
